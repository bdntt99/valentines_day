FOR MY LOVE
this valentines day ,beneath a bleeding sky
i bring no sweet confession but pure words of love and truth
draining my own heart to ink these lines
beating your name a forbidden rythm

Tonight the moonlight drapes velvet over your shoulder
black lace stitched with distant stars
your eyes enchanting
like two darkened galaxies
they pull me past the edge of reason

my pulse become a restless raven
under your Shadow
every breath you steal from me
is holy and profane at once

I am anchored in this dark gravity you exert
If loving you is a descent then I am glad to fall
spiraling through the velvet dark
until there is nothing left but the echo of your name
and the ink that finally mercifully runs dry

Do you want to be my Valentine?
